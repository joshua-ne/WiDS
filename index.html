WiDS@Santa Clara

We are excited to partner with Stanford University to bring the Women in Data Science (WiDS) conference to Santa Clara, California for the first time. WiDS Santa Clara is an independent event that coincides with the annual Global WiDS Conference held at Stanford University and an estimated 150+ locations worldwide. The event features talks by distinguished women scholars and practitioners.

The Global WiDS Conference aims to inspire and educate data scientists worldwide, regardless of gender, and to support women in the field. This technical conference brings together data scientists and professionals in related fields around the world to discuss the latest research and applications of data science in a number of domains. It also connects participants with potential mentors, collaborators, and others in the field.
